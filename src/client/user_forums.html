Join us for the 2020 nThrive Virtual User Forums

Dont miss this opportunity to gain valuable knowledge!

October 12 - 16 // November 2 - 6

2020 has brought multifaceted challenges to health care leaders. One thing the health care industry can be certain of: the next normal for health care will look nothing like the normal we are leaving behind.

Many leaders are now beginning to recognize the need to reimagine, reshape and reform their day-to-day revenue cycle operations. At nThrive, were building a timely and high-value agenda for our 2020 Virtual Client User Forums and invite you to join us for these educational opportunities.

We realize that time is one of your most valuable assets right now. Thats why were conducting our virtual sessions on two separate weeks, giving you the ability to select sessions on either or both weeks that best fit your schedule: October 12 - 16 or November 2 - 6.

During the virtual sessions, you can look forward to learning new methods and ways of thinking to:

  • Address near-term cash pressures
  • Improve payment focused on overcoming barriers with patients and payers
  • Prepare for the 2021 Price Transparency mandate
  • Leverage new product enhancements and how to influence product roadmaps and strategies
  • Engage robotic process automation to accelerate COVID-19 recovery
  • Increase your knowledge to navigate the health care landscape with best practices
  • and much more

Agenda

You can access a preview of the agenda and register today here (LINK). All sessions reflect Eastern Time.

Who should attend?

Directors, managers, supervisors and other users of nThrive solutions. Sessions are for current clients only.

Cost

There is no charge to participate.
